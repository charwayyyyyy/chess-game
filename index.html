Chess Rules

  • King moves one square in any direction
  • Queen moves any number of squares diagonally, horizontally, or vertically
  • Rook moves any number of squares horizontally or vertically
  • Bishop moves any number of squares diagonally
  • Knight moves in an L-shape (2 squares one way, 1 square perpendicular)
  • Pawn moves forward one square, captures diagonally
  • Check - king is under threat of capture
  • Checkmate - king is in check with no legal moves
  • Castling - king and rook move together for protection
  • Promotion - pawn reaching opposite side becomes queen
  • Time Control - optional time limits for each player
  • Stalemate - no legal moves left, game ends in draw
